Trending Development

Tsing Ho City Rail Transit Line 1

As the main artery of Tsing Ho City, the Tsing Ho City Rail Transit Line 1 connects diverse landscapes such as ancient city culture, natural scenery, modern city, and leisure seaside. Each station along the way incorporates unique designs, allowing you to feel the diverse charm of Tsing Ho City even inside the train. Whether exploring the history of the ancient city or enjoying a vacation at the seaside, Line 1 will take you on a colorful tour of Tsing Ho City.
